Ningaloo

Ningaloo was developed by a team of four in a week as a final project for DevBootcamp. We choose to use all new technologies (namely Meteor and MongoDB) because we needed a native mobile app in order to cache data on the phone until it was back in range.

The Ningaloo Turtle Program is an existing program in Australia where volunteers wander the beaches documenting nested turtle eggs. We worked in collaboration with this non-profit and will be beta-testing this system next season.

Wanders

Wanders is an app that connects you to beautiful public spaces. The app allows you to enter a place you want to explore or uses your geo-location to source local places of interest. Spaces are listed on the right and as you scroll through, the map will zoom in on the location and bring up an accompanying image of the space. This app has a feature that allows you to send the address to your phone via text using the Twilio api.

Wanders was a solo passion project that was developed in four days.

Pique.it

Pique.it is a project that was developed at a two day hackathon that explores the concept of revolutionizing travel search. Our idea was to create a site that focused on travel decisions based on experiencing local culture rather than being centered around tourist attractions. Pique.it maps the current most popular searches, gives you the lowest fare to that destination and provides a montage of pictures take by locals using the Flickr api giving you glimpses of local festivities, eats, sights and culture.
