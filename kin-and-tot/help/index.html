How do I find families near me?

Open the Discover tab. The map shows families in your neighborhood. Use the filter bar at the top to narrow by kids' ages. Tap any pin to see a family's profile.

How do I connect with another family?

Tap "Wave" on a family's profile. They'll get a notification and can wave back. Once you've both waved, you're connected and can message each other from the Connect tab.

What is a Pod?

A Pod is a small private group of trusted families (typically 3–6) who coordinate childcare together. Pod members can log care hours, share availability, and send quick asks to the group.

How do I create a Pod?

Go to the Pods tab and tap the + button. Give it a name, select connected families to invite, and tap Create. Invited families will receive a notification and can accept or decline.

What are Beacons?

Beacons are spontaneous playdate invitations. Post a Beacon from the Playdates tab when you're heading to a park or playground. Nearby families can see it and join you.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Delete Profile. This permanently removes your family profile, photos, messages, and all associated data.
