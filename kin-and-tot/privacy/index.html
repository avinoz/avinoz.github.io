Privacy Policy

Kin & Tot  ·  Effective June 9, 2025

Kin & Tot ("we," "our," or "the app") is a neighborhood app that helps parents and caregivers find and connect with nearby families. This policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when creating a profile:

  • Parent names — the display names shown on your family profile.
  • About your kids — nicknames, age ranges, and interest tags. Real names are optional and never shown publicly.
  • Neighborhood and location — the neighborhood name you choose and an approximate map location. We never store or share your precise GPS coordinates with other users.
  • Family photos — images you upload to your profile. Photos may be optionally processed with face-pixelation before upload.
  • Bio and vibe tags — a short description and tags that describe your family's interests and schedule.
  • Messages — messages you send to other families through the in-app chat.
  • Care hour logs — hours logged within a pod (a private group of trusted families).

We also collect technical information automatically:

  • Device push token — to deliver notifications about waves, playdate invites, and pod activity. Tokens are stored against your account and deleted when you sign out or delete your profile.
  • Apple user identifier — provided by Sign in with Apple. We use this only to identify your account and never expose it to other users.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Show your family profile to nearby families on the Discover map.
  • Facilitate connections, playdates, and pod coordination between families.
  • Deliver push notifications for app activity you have opted into.
  • Let you manage and delete your own data.

We do not use your information for advertising, sell it to third parties, or use it to build profiles for purposes unrelated to the app.

3. Who Can See Your Information

Your family profile (names, neighborhood, bio, kids' age ranges, and photos) is visible to other authenticated Kin & Tot users nearby. Messages are only visible to the families in that conversation. Pod data (care hours, availability) is only visible to the pod's members.

You can hide your profile from specific families using the Hidden Families feature in Settings.

4. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored using Supabase, which provides encrypted storage and row-level security so each user can only access data they are permitted to see. Photos are stored in Supabase Storage with access controls enforced server-side.

5. Children's Privacy

Kin & Tot is designed for parents and adult caregivers, not for use by children. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. Kids' information entered into the app (nicknames, age ranges) is provided by and managed by the parent or guardian who created the family profile.

6. Your Choices and Rights

  • Edit your profile — update any information at any time from the Profile tab.
  • Delete your profile — Settings → Delete Profile permanently removes your family profile, kids, photos, and messages from our servers.
  • Disable notifications — Settings → Notifications, or through your device's notification settings.
  • Hide families — Settings → Hidden Families lets you remove specific families from your view.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your profile, all associated data is permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact
