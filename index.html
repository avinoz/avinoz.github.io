Alvin Shiu

I've meticulously designed buildings standing in seven countries around the world; I’ve built half a dozen stages and public art pieces by hand. It’s time for a new challenge and use tech as a new medium and reach a broader audience.

With my extensive background in design, project management working with interdisciplinary teams, I am able to bring greater insight into the earlier stages of development in regards to efficiency, user-experience and branding.

VISIT ARCHITECTURAL WORK

Ningaloo

Ningaloo was developed by a team of four in a week as a final project for DevBootcamp. We choose to use all new technologies (namely Meteor and MongoDB) because we needed a native mobile app in order to cache data on the phone until it was back in range.

The Ningaloo Turtle Program is an existing program in Australia where volunteers wander the beaches documenting nested turtle eggs. We worked in collaboration with this non-profit and will be beta-testing this system next season.
